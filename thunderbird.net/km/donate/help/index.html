Our Donor Support Team is unable to respond to any technical support questions. We do not provide technical support in exchange for financial gifts. To ensure that you get the help that you need, please visit our technical support page.

Thunderbird technical support is community-based and available to everyone. Our technical support page includes knowledge base articles and a community of volunteers ready to help!

Visit our technical support site to get more help.
Our Donor Support Team is unable to respond to your Thunderbird product feedback and feature requests. But, we want to hear from you! Please visit Mozilla Connect.

Mozilla Connect is a collaborative space for ideas, feedback, and discussions that will help shape future Thunderbird product releases. Submit your ideas and be sure to tag Thunderbird so others can find and vote on your suggestions.

For Thunderbird technical support, please visit our support page

Visit Mozilla Connect to submit your ideas for Thunderbird.
To update the email that you are using to make and manage your donations, please visit our Donor Portal.

To sign in to your donor portal, please use the email that you used to make your donation.
We currently accept over 100 currencies through our donation form! If you do not see your preferred currency listed, we unfortunately cannot accept that currency at this time.

We also accept a number of payment methods which, depending upon your location, include by Credit Card, PayPal, SEPA Direct Debit, iDEAL, Google Pay, Apple Pay, and ACH. If you do not see your preferred payment method listed, we unfortunately do not accept that method at this time.

We do not accept donations in crypto.

If you are in the US (or using a US bank account), you can donate via check. Detailed instructions are available on our Donation FAQ page.
