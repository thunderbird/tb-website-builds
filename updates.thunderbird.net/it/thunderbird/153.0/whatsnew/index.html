Thunderbird 153

Welcome to Meadow. This release makes Thunderbird more capable and personal, with native Exchange support, a revised Account Hub, and customization options that help you make Thunderbird your own.

Easier Account Creation

The all new Account Hub makes setting up accounts faster and more intuitive, allowing you to connect to calendars and address books automatically.

Address Book Set Up

Meadow will scan your existing accounts and configure your local and/or remote address books within Thunderbird.

Microsoft Exchange Support

Users can now set up their Microsoft Exchange accounts natively without any add-ons to read, manage, and write emails.

Colori primari

You can also choose to customize your colors with new accent color settings in the Appearance tab.

Customize Your Notifications

Set an email as read, delete, mark as spam and more right from the native notifications on your operating system.

e molto altro ancora…

OAuth in browser

Thunderbird now supports OAuth authentication directly in your default browser.

Login With Thundermail

If you have a Thundermail account you can sign into Thunderbird with a one-click authentication in the Account Hub.

Folder Sorting Improvements

Improved UI and better visual indicators make sorting your emails easier than ever.

Correzioni di bug e miglioramenti

Corretti migliaia di bug e migliorate le prestazioni per offrirti l’esperienza Thunderbird fluida e affidabile che ti aspetti.
